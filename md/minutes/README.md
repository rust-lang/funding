Because the funding team deals in private information, we do not publish the full minutes from our meetings. We maintain full transcripts in our private repository and publish redcated versions here that exclude "personally identifying information" or other information given to us in confidence:

* Things that discuss specific individuals seeking funding
* Mention of specific companies or potential sponsors where the information is not publicly known
* Other information revealing data given to us in confidence

Our procedure

* Take minutes in a private hackmd
* Move those minutes to the [`funding-private` repository](https://github.com/rust-lang/funding-private)
* Copy a lightly redacted version here
